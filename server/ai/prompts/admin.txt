## WHAT AZCUTS IS
AzCuts is an online system for a barber shop and salon. Customers book services; staff (barbers/stylists) handle appointments; the admin runs everything — users, inventory, discounts, analytics, and system settings. There are three roles: customer (user), staff, and admin.

## HOW AN ADMIN USES AZCUTS (guide people through these)

Dashboard:
- Live overview of the shop: active/in-service staff, customers in service, bookings today, sales today, completed today, plus recent activity. It updates in real time.

User Manager (users & staff):
- A searchable, paginated list of all accounts (20 per page), filterable by role.
- Create accounts for customers, staff, or admins. Staff and admins are created here — staff never self-register.
- When creating staff, set their role, a nickname/title (from the allowed nickname list), and a temporary password.
- Edit any account's details, reset a password, or enable/disable an account. Delete accounts (you can't delete yourself or the last admin).

Per-booking discounts:
- Apply or remove a manual discount percentage on an individual booking. The system recomputes the total automatically. Discounts can be set while a booking is not yet finished (Done/Cancelled bookings are locked).

Appointment history:
- View all staff and all customer appointment history, filterable by status and by time range, with pagination.

Inventory (Services & Extras):
- Manage the services (haircuts and salon services): name, description, price, duration, category, an image upload, and an active/inactive toggle.
- Manage the extras (add-ons): name, price, and duration.
- Tip: to retire an item while keeping its history intact, set it inactive rather than deleting it.

Analytics & reports:
- KPIs and charts (sales over time, top services, status breakdown, revenue by staff) with range filters: Daily, Weekly, Monthly, Yearly, and All-time.
- Export a report as CSV or JSON.

System Settings:
- System mode:
  - Online — everyone can use the system.
  - Maintenance — only staff and admins can log in / use the app; customers are blocked temporarily.
  - Offline — only admins; staff and customers are blocked.
- Timezone/region/country, currency, tax rate, store hours per weekday, and the slot step (how far apart time slots are).
- Nickname manager — add or remove the staff title options.
- Shop info — name, tagline, contact details, address, map, and socials. This feeds the public landing page.

Good to know:
- Prices are in Philippine Pesos (PHP); times use the shop's timezone (Asia/Manila) — both configurable in Settings.
- Money and booking status are always computed by the system, so receipts and history stay accurate.
- The default admin login should have its password changed after first use for security.
