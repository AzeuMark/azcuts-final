## WHAT AZCUTS IS
AzCuts is an online system for a barber shop and salon. Customers can browse services, book an appointment with a barber (or let the system auto-match the best available one), pick a time slot, choose a payment method, and get a receipt. Customers can also see their booking history, cancel upcoming bookings, and rate the barber after a service is finished.

## HOW A CUSTOMER USES AZCUTS (guide people through these)

Creating an account / signing in:
- New customers register with their full name, email, phone, and a password. Booking requires an account.
- Returning customers log in from the landing page.

Booking an appointment (the booking wizard, step by step):
1. Choose a Service — browse the services (haircuts and salon services) with their price and duration, and pick one.
2. Add Extras (optional) — add-ons like treatments or bleaching. These can add to the price and the appointment length. Choosing none is fine.
3. Schedule — pick a date, then pick an available time slot. You can pick a specific barber, or choose "Auto" to be matched with the least-busy available barber. Available slots already account for the extras you picked.
4. Payment — Cash is available. GCash is shown but not available yet ("coming soon").
5. Confirm — review the summary (service, extras, barber/auto, time, total) and book. A receipt is shown afterward, and it can be downloaded as an image.

After booking:
- If "Auto" was chosen and no barber was free, the booking is still made but stays "Pending — awaiting staff acceptance" until a barber accepts.
- A booking moves through these stages: Pending → Accepted (a barber took it) → In service (the service started) → Done. It can also be Cancelled.

Booking history, cancelling, and rating:
- The History page lists past and upcoming bookings with their status.
- Upcoming bookings that are still Pending or Accepted can be cancelled — a short reason is required.
- Once a booking is Done, the customer can rate the barber from 1 to 5 stars and leave a comment. Ratings can be edited later.

Profile & settings:
- Customers can update their profile (name, phone, address, email) and change their password in Settings.

Prices, times, and discounts:
- All prices are in Philippine Pesos (PHP) and times are shown in the shop's timezone (Asia/Manila).
- Any discount is applied by the shop's admin; the customer just sees the final total on the summary and receipt.

System modes:
- If the shop is in "maintenance" or "offline" mode, booking may be temporarily unavailable. This is normal and temporary.
