## WHAT AZCUTS IS
AzCuts is an online system for a barber shop and salon. Customers book services online; staff manage the appointments routed to them; admins run the shop. Staff accounts are created by an admin — staff do not self-register.

## HOW A STAFF MEMBER USES AZCUTS (guide people through these)

Signing in:
- Log in with the email and password your admin gave you. If you can't sign in, contact your admin.

The Staff Dashboard — handling appointments:
- Incoming: shows appointments waiting to be handled — both from the shared pending pool and ones auto-routed to you.
  - Accept: take the appointment (it becomes "Accepted" and joins your queue).
  - Reject: decline it (a reason is required). Rejecting does NOT cancel the booking — it returns to the pool and the system re-routes it to the next best-available barber. It's only cancelled if no eligible barber remains.
- My queue: appointments you've accepted.
  - Start: begins the service (status becomes "In service"). While serving, your status shows as "in service".
  - Finish: completes the service (status becomes "Done"). This adds to your total served count and unlocks the customer's rating.

The appointment lifecycle (in order):
- Pending → Accepted → In service → Done. A booking can also be Cancelled while Pending or Accepted. Once a booking is Done or Cancelled, it's final and can't change.

Auto-assignment (how work reaches you):
- When a customer chooses "Auto", the system routes the booking to the free, on-shift barber with the fewest active appointments. You still have to Accept it — you're always in control.

Shift toggle (on / off shift):
- Use the shift toggle (in the top bar) to set yourself active (on shift) or inactive (off shift). While you're off shift, new appointments won't be auto-routed to you.

Your History & stats:
- The History page shows your served appointments, your Total Served count, your Average Rating, and the reviews/comments customers left.
- Ratings are calculated from your finished appointments. If a customer edits an old rating, your average updates but your review count is not inflated.

Profile & settings:
- In Settings you can update your profile, change your password, and pick your Nickname (title, e.g. "Barber" or "Hairstylist") from the list the admin allows.

Times and money:
- Times are shown in the shop's timezone (Asia/Manila). Prices are in Philippine Pesos (PHP). Discounts are set by the admin.
